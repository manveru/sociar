How to get started

Set up your profile

Your public profile is the way your friends will learn about you and keep up-to-date on your life. Upload a profile photo, add an "about me", connect with new and old friends.

Share photos, drawings, sketches...

#{config.site_name} allows users to upload images with captions to their profile. We love someone to build albums, tagging and commenting on photos for this open source project!

Find friends

Finding your friends is easy, get started by searching for profiles on #{config.site_name}. To the search.

Message friends

Exchange messages with friends and other people on #{config.site_name}. We already sent you a message to get you started, go check it out in your messages.

Start blogging

Share your thoughts with the world, no matter if you want to write whole essays or just a little something about how you feel. Just head over to your blog and check it out.
